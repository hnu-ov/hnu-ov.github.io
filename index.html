Perceiving the unseen in dynamic worlds, empowering AI to adapt and explore without bounds.

I am an Associate Researcher in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Hainan University. My research interestes are centered around Open-world Vision perception, with broad interests in the areas of Zero-Shot Learning, Large Multimodal Model, Embodied AI, Remote Sensing Image, Autonomous Driving, and their applications in real-world scenarios.

I received my Ph.D. degree in 2022 from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Xiao Bai. Before that, I obtained the B.Sc degree from the School of Mathematical Sciences, Beihang University at 2016. From 2022 to 2024, I used to work as a lecturer at the School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Wuhan University of Technology. I serve as a reviewer for the top conferences and journals such as CVPR, ICCV, AAAI, AI, PR, INFFUS, ESWA etc.


Our group is always looking for highly self-motivated PhD/Master students, visiting students, and undergraduate interns. If you are interested in our research direction, please send me an email with your CV.
